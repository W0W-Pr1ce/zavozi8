Home/ Services

We have been supplying more than 30 years as suppliers of red beef from Spanish restaurants and hotels of great prestige. We currently supply nearly 3,000 HORECA channel customers throughout the peninsula, the Balearic Islands and the Canary Islands, and export to countries such as Italy, Belgium, the United Kingdom and the Netherlands, among others.

We serve in the best restaurants. Trasacar, provider of reference in quality and service.

We have weekly national routes made with own delivery vans that allow us to cover an excellent personalized service to our customers.

Seven reasons to trust Trasacar.

Do you have a restaurant? Here are seven reasons to buy meat.

We are the leading company in the red meat sector in Spain. We sell to restaurants that have been trusting us for more than thirty years and throughout our history we have never left any customer without a supply.

We keep the same quality line every week of the year with the most competitive prices in the market.

We make a great variety of cuts of meat to adapt to the consumption and profile of any client.

A phone call, an email or a whatsapp are enough to place an order that we will serve within a maximum period of 72 hours anywhere in the peninsula.

We always have enough stock of meat in our warehouses, which allows us to meet the guaranteed delivery deadlines.

Our customer service is able to solve instantly any problem that comes along with calling our customers once a week to take the order.

Our meat suppliers are the slaughterhouses and cutting plants that enjoy greater prestige within the European Economic Community.
