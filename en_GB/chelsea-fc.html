Sani Academies

Football Academy

IN ASSOCIATION WITH CHELSEA FCF

Sani shoots to score with football coaching sessions in association with 2017 Premier League champions, and one of the world’s leading football clubs, Chelsea Football Club. Yes, there’s a special treat in store for football fans with Chelsea FC Foundation coaching available to motivate, educate and inspire. It really is in a league of its own, offering an outstanding football development programme with a UEFA approved football pitch and a state of the art Club House.

The training camp is held five days a week and promises lots of active fun for children of ALL abilities under the careful eyes of qualified coaches. They’ll motivate, educate and inspire while covering skills from dribbling to shooting, as well as organising five-a-side games.;

It’s another fabulous opportunity to pick up new skills at Sani and make your visit even more worthwhile - it’s also a great way to make new friends and learn to work as part of a team.

  • Hours of operation 09:30 – 19:30
  • Sessions 09:30 – 11:30 & 17:00 – 19:00
  •  Age Groups: 4-6 years, 7-9 years, 10-12 years, 13-16 years
  • Ratio 2:12
  • Reservations to be made through the Sani website, Mobile Application and through hotels receptions
  • Related Activities Official Chelsea FC Foundation welcome kit will be offered (uniforms, bag, custom t-shirt)

Chelsea FC Foundation Football Coaching availability is limited due to space and care ratios. We strongly advise pre-booking to avoid disappointment.
