The Resort

Concept

It's easy to let yourself be carried away by the sheer beauty of Sani. A privately developed ecological reserve offering one thousand acres of natural forests, wetlands and stunning beaches. A vast space providing the freedom for you to do as you feel, whether it's to hike, run, cycle, swim or enjoy a myriad of activities.

An environment matched by the hotels themselves; discretely luxurious, spacious, contemporary, with a remarkable attention to detail and courteous, personalised service that's remembered by all who stay with us. With all of this bound together by a unique philosophy that guides everything we do and ensures this very special place will be there for future generations.

The cuisine is unrivalled, with a remarkable choice of award-winning restaurants taking diners on a culinary adventure across the Mediterranean and far beyond. And there's those unique Sani touches, such as the innovative dine around programme, a vast array of children's activities and amenities, and adult-only quiet zones.

At Sani there's simply so much to do. It’s where entertainment and activities abound, whatever your age. It's a place where people flock for the bird life, where music lovers come together for Sani Festival and where foodies mix with Michelin-starred chefs. And it's a destination unlike anything you've experienced before.
