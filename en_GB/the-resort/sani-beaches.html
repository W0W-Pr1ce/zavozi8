The Resort

Our Beaches

The finest beaches in the Mediterranean with luxury beachside service and care. Sani Resort is home to 7 exclusive beaches, each with their own style of paradise. Enjoy fresh beach refreshments aside the shimmering, azure waters as your family relax in the shallows. There are dedicated personnel to look after the little ones, too, our Babewatch Service is complimentary for up to 30 minutes while you take a break.

Read more about the Sani Beach Experience

Bousoulas beach

Four miles of immaculate pine-fringed sands with crystal clear, shallow water perfect for snorkelling. Bousoulas Beach is within easy access of all Sani Resort hotels and is EU Blue Flag awarded. 

Babewatch service available.

Sani Club beach

Turquoise waters and soft white sands make the Sani Club Beach irresistible. This luxurious, intimate beach boasts clear waters for underwater exploring and a dedicated watersports centre. 

Babewatch service available.

Sea You beach

Exclusive to Porto Sani guests, this paradise is just a few steps from your suite. With special beach amenities and services, Porto Sani guests are treated to the VIP beach experience.

Babewatch service available.

Sani Asterias beach

A haven of relaxation for adults and children with silky soft white sands right at your doorstep. Towels, refreshments and luxury sun loungers await beside tranquil, turquoise waters.

Babewatch service available. 

Ammos beach

A beautiful setting just steps from the Watersports Centre, Ammos Beach is perfectly suited for a day spent in the sun or those evening cocktails by sunset.

Babewatch service available.

Sani Hill beach

Beachfront Suite guests at Sani Beach enjoy direct access to this utopia from their own private garden. With beachside service for drinks and nibbles from Bousoulas beach bar, also famous for its white night parties.

Sani Dunes beach

A white sandy paradise, just a few steps from your private garden in Sani Dunes. As with all Sani Resort beaches, extensive food and beverage services ensure a comfortable, relaxing experience.
