Sani Academies

Kids can have fun, make friends and learn a new skill with Sani Resort sports Academies. In a fun and safe environment, our Sani Academies offer our young guests the opportunity to learn and play with our experienced coaches and instructors, who will provide them with all technical knowledge, as well as a sense of hard work and team play.

  • BEAR GRYLLS SURVIVAL ACADEMY

    IN ASSOCIATION WITH BEAR GRYLLS SURVIVAL ACADEMY

    A once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to learn expert survival skills within the unique natural surroundings of Sani resort. With the guidance of Bear Grylls’ hand-picked team of instructors, you’ll build shelters and fires, learn to navigate and signal for help. This expedition is an exhilarating way to spend quality, active time with your family, an adventure you’re sure never to forget. Learn Astro Navigation on your night walk through the woodlands then return to camp where you’ll enjoy fresh fish for dinner and toast marshmallows on the fire. Read more
