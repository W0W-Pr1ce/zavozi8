Your Stay

Entertainment

Sunny days, star-filled nights

Classically trained dancers, singers and actors, internationally recognised DJs and highly respected musicians; Sani brings together a cast of brilliant performers and entertainers from around the world.

Whatever you like, there's so much to make you happy. Take a seat in the open air theatre for a musical extravaganza, try your hand at traditional Greek dancing, hit the dance floor at one of our beach parties or simply relax to the timeless tunes of our resident pianists. With a few lovely surprises always waiting to delight you too.

At Sani, it's all about taking it easy, having fun and enjoying great company. You'll find so much happening right across the resort in a multitude of venues from our amazing state-of-the-art outdoor theatre to lobby lounges and beach bars. You can find out what's on in our online guide or simply ask one of our team. One thing's for certain, whatever you choose, you'll have a lot of fun.

Around Sani

a paradise to be discovered

view more

  • White Nights

    These memorable nights at Bousoulas Beach are engraved in Sani folklore. Great sounds, perfectly mixed cocktails and everyone having so much fun, get ready for the best beach parties ever.

  • Dance Dance Dance

    Latin, disco, funk and pop; we dare you to stay off the dance floor at our party event of the year. With refreshments flowing and the temperature rising, it's always an unforgettable night under the stars.

  • Greek Nights

    Enjoy the authentic tastes and sounds of traditional Greece at one our tremendously popular twice-weekly Greek nights. These not-to-be-missed events take place in the wonderful surroundings of Ouzerie.

  • Garden Theatre

    From the Magic of Musicals to our Michael Jackson tribute, our top entertainers put on a show to remember five evenings every week under the stars in our state-of-the-art open air Garden  Theatre.

  • Caribbean Nights

    Hot hot hot! You'll love the rhythms our Caribbean parties at Sani Beach, as our fantastic live band sets pulses racing with the sensuous sounds of Salsa, Bachata and Kizomba.

  • Live music

    You'll find a brilliant programme of live music in fabulous venues across Sani. From blues bands and fun tributes to our ever popular solo pianists, there's music to suit your mood every evening.

  • Leading DJs

    Let your hair down and hit the dance floor as our local and international DJs provide a fantastic mix of house, hip-hop, R&B and so much more.
