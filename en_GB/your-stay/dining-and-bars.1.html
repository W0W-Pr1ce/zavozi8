  • El Puerto

    A warm welcome to a unique Mediterranean dining experience. Michelin-starred chef David Ibarboure and his team invite you to enjoy the authentic taste of Spain with a modern twist. Our menus use the finest local ingredients and offer a selection of wines from some of Europe’s most renowned producers. Whether sharing tapas at the bar or going a la carte on the spacious veranda with its spectacular views, family-friendly El Puerto turns every occasion into a celebration. With Vegetarian and Gluten Free options available.

  • Water Restaurant

    Sani Asterias is proud to cooperate with Three Michelin-starred chef, Mauro Colagreco, owner of Mirazur Restaurant, ranked as the top restaurant worldwide  for 2019 in S.Pellegrino’s  World’s 50 Best Restaurants list.

    Chef Mauro Colagreco was also awarded “Chef of the Year” by the prestigious Gault & Millau restaurant guide and he received his third Michelin Star in 2019.

    Offering a distinctively Mediterranean and French focus, this unique menu selection has been carefully created to satisfy the most discerning palates. All perfectly matched by a range of fine wines sourced from Greece as well as across the globe.

    Location Sani Asterias

    Opening hours

    19:00-23:00 (reservation required)

  • Tomata Restaurant

    With a reputation stretching across Greece and far beyond, the renowned Chef Karamolegos and our team promise you an unforgettable dining experience. Offering a fiesta of colours, aromas and tastes, they bring you an inspiring Mediterranean menu with a truly innovative twist. You can also look forward to meat and fish specialties freshly barbecued by your table, together with a wine list featuring some of Greece's finest labels. Find out more

    Location Sani Marina

  • Katsu Restaurant

    Named in honour of Chef Katsuhiko Hanamure, Katsu treats you to a sumptuous array of sushi and sashimi, together with a wide array of enticing signature dishes. Reflecting a wealth of experience gained in some of the most renowned Matsuhisa and Nobu restaurants across the globe, Chef Katsu's specially created menu will delight those with a love of Japanese cuisine.  

    Location Sani Marina
