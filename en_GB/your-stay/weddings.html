Your Stay

Weddings

The most important day of your life deserves one of the most beautiful settings and Sani also offers a very warm welcome overflowing with Greek hospitality. We also have many years of experience in organising wedding celebrations for happy couples from Greece and across Europe.

Each wedding is a very individual occasion and so we recommend you speak with our wedding planner, Anna, who will talk you through the organisation and everything that Sani can offer including arrangements, accommodation, cuisine and costs. Anna will work very closely with you to ensure your day is very special indeed. However big your wedding party, you will find Sani offers everything you can dream of to make it a success and an occasion you will cherish forever.

Our wedding special includes:

  • Administration
  • Reservation of the wedding date and time at the local Town Hall
  • Welcome in room bottle of sparkling wine
  • Wedding cake for 10 people
  • Flowers in room on wedding day

To find out more contact Anna directly by email  or dial +30 23740 99513
