Sani Marina

Port Facilities

If safety and comfort is what you are looking for, Sani Marina is the ideal five-star holiday destination for you and your yacht,  awarded every year with the European Union’s "Blue Flag" for its crystal clear waters.

The Sani harbour offers the latest technology and all the facilities that are needed to provide the highest level of comfort and service all year round. You will find all the technical specifications below:

> Sani Marina is a five-star hotel for yachts, extended in 2003
> The perfect place to moor your boat all year round
> Ideal base for exploring secluded coves in Halkidiki, the Aegean Sea and its islands
> 32,000 m2 with water depth of 3m
> 215 individual berths for craft up to 33m in length
> Pontoons 3m wide, with attached finger piers
> Providing power supply, drinking water, fuel, sewerage and fire protection piping
> Traffic-free roadway around the Marina
> Fully protected by 24-hour Sani Resort security services
> Boat maintenance - on request
> Boat chartering service


General Information
Location Kassandra, Halkidiki, Greece
(80 km from Thessaloniki airport)
Geographic latitude 40ο 05’ 846 Ν
Geographic longitude 23ο 18’ 374 Ε
Capacity Berths 215 boats
Depth 3 meters / 10 ft
Boat lengths 5 - 33m / 16 - 110 ft
(mooring or fingers)
VHF contact Channel 9
Marina entry Two green and two red lights mark
the channel into the marina. When
sailing in, please switch to Channel 9
or call +30.23740.99.581 -2
for advice on navigation.
