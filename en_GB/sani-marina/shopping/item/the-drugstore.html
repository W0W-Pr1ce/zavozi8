Sani Marina Shopping

A hidden gem, the Sani Marina piazza is a luxurious destination promising a wealth of international brands in an elegant setting. Whether you are looking for the latest beachwear or something guaranteed to get you noticed in the evening, that perfect present or a piece of jewellery that will be cherished forever, the Piazza offers all you need.

Take your time and treat yourself to a spot of very special retail therapy before taking your pick from a choice of magnificent restaurants offering some of the finest cuisine you’ll find in the Mediterranean.

    BEAUTY, WELLNESS & PERSONAL CARE

    The health & beauty store of the Marina is the absolute destination if you want to look and feel better than ever before. Here you can find pharmaceutical products, Greek natural cosmetics from top brands and innovative unique products like Fillerina, created by the leading Swiss cosmetic treatment company Labo. Fillerina visibly improves facial anatomy without deforming it. It is an easy-to-use dermocosmetic treatment that reverses the consequences of reduced hyaluronic acid deposits on the skin, which is observed over time.
