Sani Marina Dining & Bars

When it comes to a fabulous evening out, the restaurants and bars of the Piazza offer all you need. Get your evening off to a lively start with an aperitif at one of the exciting selection of bars before moving on to enjoy one of the mouthwatering menus ranging from inspired sushi to traditional Greek. And after dinner just sit back with a drink and enjoy the company to the early hours.

Sani Marina is a short walk from Sani Beach, Sani Asterias and Porto Sani. The best way for Sani Club guests to access Sani Marina is by shuttle bus (available every 20 minutes).
