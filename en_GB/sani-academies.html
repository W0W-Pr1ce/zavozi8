Sani Academies

Sani Football Academy in association with Chelsea FC Foundation

Sani takes the lead once again with a fantastic move. All our younger guests can enjoy the opportunity to train with Chelsea FC Foundation - the 2017 Premier League champions

and one of the world’s leading football clubs. It’s a fabulous chance for them to improve their soccer skills with the professionals - and just another way in which Sani adds value to your family holiday.

Any budding premiership players aged 4-16 years can join in the training at our Chelsea FC Foundation Football Coaching. It offers an outstanding football development programme with a UEFA-approved football pitch and a state of the art Club House. 

There will be a training camp held five days a week that promises lots of active fun for children of all abilities under the careful eyes of professional coaches. They’ll cover skills from dribbling to shooting, as well as organizing five-a-side games and is a great way for kids to make new friends and learn to work as part of a team.

  • Hours of operation 09:30 – 19:30
  • Sessions 09:30 – 11:30 & 17:00 – 19:00
  • Age Groups: 4-6 years, 7-9 years, 10-12 years, 13-16 years
  • Ratio 2:12
  • Reservations to be made through the Sani website, Mobile Application and through hotels receptions
  • Related Activities Official Chelsea welcome kit will be offered (uniforms, bag, custom t-shirt)

You can pre-book here: CHELSEA FC FOUNDATION FOOTBALL COACHING

Sani Dance Academy

The Sani  Dance Academy operates from April to October.

Children have the opportunity to learn to dance, gain confidence, enhance their creativity and form friendships. We teach a range of styles from Latin to hip-hop or modern to break-dancing.

Our dance professionals are DBS checked, First-Aid trained and hold a dance teaching qualification - ‘IDTA Associate Freestyle Dance Teaching Qualification’ – which is an internationally recognized qualification.

The Dance Academy  is open from Monday to Saturday.

Ratios

One teacher to twelve children in one session.

Schedule

  • Monday: Jazz Dance
  • Tuesday: Street Dance
  • Wednesday: Cheerleading
  • Thursday: Latin & Salsa
  • Friday: Lyrical Dance
  • Saturday: Show Day

Booking

Guests can pre-book online or register in resort.

Costs

18 Months – 3 Years €10 per session

4-6 Years €15 per session

7-11 Years €20 per session

12-18 Years €20 per session

Sani Marina Sailing Academy

Sani Marina, in cooperation with the Nautical Club of Thessaloniki invites children, teenagers and adults alike to experience the initial steps of Sailing in 9-hour intensive courses, while keen dinghy sailors can further strengthen their dexterity under the attentive supervision of our skilled instructors.

Lessons are held both on dry land and off shore and the participants are awarded with the accredited “Certificate of Attendance”.

Duration: May - August

Sailing Session Dates: Tuesday-Thursday, Friday-Sunday

Session Hours: 09:00 – 12:00; 13:00 – 16:00; 16:30 – 19:30

Cost: 285€ per pupil per 9-hour course

Family (2+ pupils): 255€ per pupil per 9-hour course
