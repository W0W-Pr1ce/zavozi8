A supreme renovation of Porto Sani launches in 2020, with new restaurants, a new spa and high-end upgrades in services. Introducing 98 completely refurbished, luxury suites, each fully tailored to babies, children and their parents. The new Porto Sani promises a completely personalized holiday, perfectly catering to the needs of young families. Porto Sani guests benefit from an exclusive, complimentary session with Carol Mae Baby Consulting Services.

An ideal location in the heart of Sani, everything is located on the Porto Sani doorstep, just footsteps away from award-winning restaurants, boutique shopping and private sandy beaches. With Free Full Board eligible guests enjoy a new world of culinary offering that includes the magnificent  ‘El Puerto’ – Porto Sani’s modern Spanish restaurant, led by Michelin Star Chef David Ibarboure – as well as an enhanced Dine Around programme that offers dining options at more than 24 restaurants across Sani. Little Guests will take a culinary journey of their own with children’s food stations and special menus for babies and children from world-leading food expert, Annabel Karmel.
