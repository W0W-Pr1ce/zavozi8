Exceptionally spacious family suites offer the highest level of luxury and privacy to larger families with three separate bedrooms, private living room including dining corner and three bathrooms with luxury bathroom toiletries by Anne Semonin, coffee and tea making facilities, Nespresso machine, mini bar, Dream Beds and pillow selection for extra comfort, international direct-dial telephone, 4 flat screen televisions with satellite multi-channel and integrated music channels, DVD / CD player, complimentary wireless internet access, private garden and their own extensive outdoor pools (45 m2), complete with luxury garden furniture (Indoor space approx. 180 m2, exterior areas 310 m2 / 210 m2 of private garden and terrace and 100 m2 backyard).

The Three Bedroom Deluxe Suites with private heated pool can accommodate up to 7 people; a maximum of 6 adults or 2 adults and 5 children up to the age of 12.
