Sani Dunes

Rooms

Sani Dunes offers 146 magnificent rooms, each one welcoming guests with unique contemporary style and everything needed for a very memorable stay.

The 50 open plan suites and 56 spacious rooms offer understated luxury and comfort, while 40 Deluxe One Bedroom suites Grand Balcony and One Bedroom suites Grand Balcony additionally feature an interior sliding door, dividing bedroom from the seating area for extra privacy.

From 24 hour room service and complimentary Wi-Fi to Nespresso machines and the latest technology, everything is perfectly in place.

Guests staying at the deluxe suites can enjoy the hotel’s private beach, just a few meters away from their doorstep. All deluxe suites feature a sophisticated and elegant  design, with subtle touches of luxury throughout.

All Sani Dunes suite guests  benefit from a complimentary private tour across the beautiful surroundings of Kassandra Peninsula in a luxurious chauffeur-driven Volvo limousine: an exclusive partnership between Sani and Volvo for all Sani Resort suite guests.

Sani Resort offers you the possibility to book your accommodation and your flight to Sani Resort at one place with access to the most affordable flights including low cost and charter options.

Book online now and get best rate guarantee as well as free transfers from and to Thessaloniki airport (offer available for stays of 5 nights or more).
