Sani Dunes

Sports & Activities

Sani Dunes brings guests a full choice of sports and outdoor activities to ensure every stay is a real pleasure. At Sani Sports Centre six tennis courts are available day and night together with many other activities including  table tennis, beach volley ball and mini soccer. There's a full range of water sports to enjoy including windsurfing and waterskiing, as well as  fun activities such as pedalos and ringos.

The open-air Garden Theatre provides a full schedule of entertainment, musicals and shows, while each year Sani plays host to the Sani Festival - one of the Mediterranean's leading musical celebrations. No stay is complete without a sundowner aboard our sunset cruise.

  • BIRD WATCHING

    Discover an amazing array of birds on a private tour of the magical Sani Wetlands, home to almost half of the bird species found in Greece. Expert eco-guides take you on an amazing 2,5km trail - a complimentary walk offered twice a week. Sani has worked closely with the University of the Aegean to document over 215 types of birds, many of them rare and endangered, as well as developing a plan for their protection with experts from the Hellenic Ornithological Society (partners of BirdLife International, the world’s largest nature conservation Partnership). All guests joining our tour will also receive a complimentary copy of the fabulous Sani Wetlands coffee table book.

    Pricing
    Free

  • FOREST WALKS

    Explore the local area and enjoy kilometres of excellently-maintained forest trails, a fabulous way to enjoy our surroundings and be at one with nature. A wide variety of walks are available from a short stroll to day trips taking in the flora, wildlife and ancient history, much of it unique to Halkidiki. You’ll enjoy beautiful views across the wetlands and bird sanctuary. Maps for these hikes are available at your hotel reception and guided tours are also offered on weekly basis, with our experienced eco-guide.

    Pricing
    Free

  • CHAUFFEUR - DRIVEN ADVENTURE

    In conjunction with Volvo, Sani takes you on an exciting experience across the Kassandra Peninsula. This professional chauffeur-driven private tour presents all kinds of fabulous photo opportunities. Exclusively for all suite guests at Sani Resort*, this complimentary two-hour adventure takes you down forest tracks, through olive groves and along dramatic cliff top paths. The highlight of the trip is a stop at the tip of Cape Posidi where guests are treated to magnificent views of the Aegean and Mount Olympus accompanied by a glass of sparkling wine. 

    *“Chauffeur Drive Adventure” applies to guests staying in one, two and three-bedroom suites at Sani Beach & Sani Club, in all deluxe one bedroom and  deluxe beachfront suites of Sani Dunes and all suites of Porto Sani & Sani Asterias.

    Pricing
    Free
