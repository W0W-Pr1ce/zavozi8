Sani Club Sports & Activities

All the family can enjoy their favourite holiday activities and discover some new ones at the Sani Club. Keep yourself in great shape in the fitness studio or perhaps take to the water at the watersports centre for some PADI scuba diving or wake boarding.

There’s lots of organised activities too such as trekking and archery, or, if you want to chill out to some great sounds join one of our beach parties.
